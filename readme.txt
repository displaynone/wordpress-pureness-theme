Pureness Theme Installation Instructions:


1. Screenshot Tutorial: http://bit.ly/RnDY6e (Dropbox)
2. Unpack or unzip Pureness.
3. Log in to the WordPress Administration Dashboard.
4. Upload the /pureness folder to your /wp-content/themes directory.
5. Click 'Themes' sub-menu from 'Appearance' menu.
6. Activate the Pureness theme from the available list of themes to preview.
7. Click on the Pureness Options menu item.
